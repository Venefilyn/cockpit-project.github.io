Cockpit 212 and Cockpit-podman 13

by Gundersanne – Feb 5, 2020
category: release

Cockpit is the modern Linux admin interface. We release regularly. Here are the release notes from version 212.

Per-page documentation

The help menu now includes links to page- and OS-specific documentation.

Localize times

The Overview, Accounts, and Logs pages now show dates and times in the correctly-localized format.

Podman: Show full log

Show containers’ logs since their startup, not just since opening the Logs tab.
