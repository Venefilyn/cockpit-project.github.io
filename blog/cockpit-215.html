Cockpit 215

by Katerina Koukiou – Mar 18, 2020
category: release

Cockpit is the modern Linux admin interface. We release regularly. Here are the release notes from version 215.

Networking: Show additional included ports in firewall zones

The Firewall page now shows ports that are directly added to a zone at the end of the services list.

Docker: Deprecate cockpit-docker in favor of cockpit-podman

cockpit-docker is being deprecated in favor of cockpit-podman, and will not be maintained upstream. We have stopped building it for Ubuntu ≥ 20.04, Debian ≥ 11, and Fedora >= 32 (i. e. the current development series), but will keep it for the stable releases (i. e. backports).
